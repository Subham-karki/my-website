Trekking
Trekking is a long-distance,multi-day journey on foot through natural, often remote or rugged terrain such as mountains, forests, or wilderness areas. Unlike short recreational hikes, trekking typically lasts several days to weeks, involves sustained physical effort, and requires detailed planning, specialized gear, and overnight stays in camps, mountain lodges, or tea houses. It often takes place off established trails, in high-altitude environments, and demands greater physical endurance and mental preparation.
