About Us

Nepstro is a cozy and inviting cafe located in the heart of the city. We are passionate about providing our customers with a warm and welcoming atmosphere where they can relax, unwind, and enjoy delicious food and beverages.Our menu features a variety of coffee blends, teas, and delectable pastries that are made with love and care.
At Nepstro, we believe in creating a sense of community and connection, making it the perfect place to gather with friends, work on your laptop, or simply enjoy a quiet moment with a cup of your favorite brew. We are committed to delivering exceptional service and quality products to ensure that every visit to Nepstro is a memorable one.
Nepstro is located in the bustling Trade Tower in Thapathali, Kathmandu. Our central location makes it easily accessible for both locals and tourists looking for a cozy spot to enjoy a cup of tea or coffee. Whether you're in the mood for a quick break from shopping or a relaxing place to catch up with friends, Nepstro is the perfect destination.
